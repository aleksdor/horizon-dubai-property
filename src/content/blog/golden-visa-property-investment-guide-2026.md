# Golden Visa Through Dubai Property: Everything You Need to Know in 2026

The UAE Golden Visa has become one of the world's most attractive residency-by-investment programs. For property investors, it offers a straightforward path to a 10-year renewable residency in one of the world's safest, most tax-friendly jurisdictions.

## What Changed in 2026?

The UAE government has continued to streamline the Golden Visa process. Key updates:

- **Digital application:** Most of the process can now be completed online through the ICP smart platform
- **Faster processing:** Average processing time reduced to 2-3 weeks
- **Multiple properties:** Combining properties to reach the AED 2M threshold is fully supported
- **Off-plan accepted:** Properties from RERA-approved developers now qualify even during construction

## The AED 2,000,000 Threshold

The minimum property investment for Golden Visa eligibility is AED 2,000,000 (approximately $545,000 USD). This can be:

- A single property worth AED 2M+
- Multiple properties totaling AED 2M+
- A mortgaged property where your equity is AED 2M+

### What Counts Toward the Threshold

| Counts ✓ | Does Not Count ✗ |
|----------|------------------|
| Purchase price on title deed | Furniture or fit-out costs |
| Multiple freehold properties combined | Properties in non-freehold zones |
| Off-plan from RERA-approved developers | Lease agreements |
| Ready properties with title deed | Properties under AED 750K individually (for combining) |

## Best Areas for Golden Visa Properties

Not all areas offer the same value at the AED 2M price point. Here's where your money goes furthest:

### Downtown Dubai
At AED 2M, expect a quality 1-bedroom apartment with Burj Khalifa views. Strong rental demand, 4-6% yields, and excellent appreciation potential.

### Dubai Marina
AED 2M gets you a spacious 2-bedroom with marina views. One of Dubai's most liquid markets — easy to rent and easy to sell.

### Business Bay
Emerging as Downtown's more affordable neighbor. AED 2M buys a premium 2-bedroom with canal views. Yields of 6-7%.

### Dubai Hills Estate
For families, AED 2M can stretch to a small townhouse or a premium apartment. Green community, excellent schools nearby.

## The Application Process

1. **Purchase property** and complete DLD registration
2. **Obtain title deed** from Dubai Land Department
3. **Gather documents:** passport, photos, health insurance, title deed
4. **Apply online** through ICP smart platform or visit GDRFA
5. **Medical fitness test** and biometrics
6. **Receive Golden Visa** (stamped in passport)
7. **Apply for family members** simultaneously or after

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| DLD registration | 4% of property value |
| Agency commission | 2% |
| Golden Visa application | AED 4,000-6,000 |
| Medical test | AED 500 |
| Emirates ID | AED 370 |
| Health insurance (annual) | AED 3,000-10,000 |

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Not checking developer RERA registration** for off-plan purchases
2. **Ignoring service charges** — they vary dramatically between buildings
3. **Buying just to meet the threshold** without considering rental potential
4. **Not factoring in DLD fees** when budgeting (adds 4% to purchase price)
5. **Rushing the process** — take time to view multiple properties and compare
